About me


I am a Finnish-Tunisian web-development student. I have moved to Helsinki in 2012 having spent my childhood in Tunisia. I previously studied translation and have been working as a translator for a over 3 years.

My abundant energy fuels me in the pursuit of many interests and hobbies. I like to immerse myself in video games, and I am very passionate about gaming. I also like to juggle different projects and learn different areas of studies. I like to keep learning and to always challenge myself by doing things that matter. I am a music enthusiast, traveler, I love to read books and to skateboard.
